     - software engineering is a systemetic approch to the design, devlopment, operation, and maintenance of a software system.

        SDLC Phases :- 1. Requirement Collection/Gathering
                  	                 2. Analsis
	                 3. Design
	                 4. Implementation
	                 5. Testing
	                 6. Maintenance
